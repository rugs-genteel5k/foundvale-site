04 / Straight answers

Frequently asked questions

Do you guarantee rankings or AI citations?+
No — and you should be skeptical of anyone who does. AI engines change constantly. Our scope is defined by deliverables: audits, articles, page specs, schema, and the monthly Snapshot. You judge the work every month, and you can leave with 30 days' notice.
Do you use AI to produce the content?+
Yes — AI-leveraged production with human review on everything that ships. Every technical claim — spec, tolerance, material grade, certification — is sourced from your questionnaire answers or your published materials. Anything unsourced doesn't ship.
How much of our team's time does this take?+
About 30 minutes in month one for the questionnaire, then a few minutes a month. No standing calls. Discovery and debrief calls are scheduled at booking.
What if we don't get the questionnaire back to you one month?+
Nothing stalls and nothing gets invented. We substitute topics we already have verified input for — it's written into the agreement.
Who owns the content?+
You do. All IP transfers on payment. Everything is delivered in editable formats your team publishes on your own site.
Why is the pilot only $500?+
It's deliberately small — a fixed two-week scope so you can judge us on actual work product instead of promises. The fee is credited to your first retainer month if you continue.
What kinds of companies is this for?+
Specialty manufacturers and industrial distributors, typically $2–25M revenue, with little or no in-house marketing staff. If you're bigger and fully staffed, an enterprise agency is probably a better fit — we'll say so.
